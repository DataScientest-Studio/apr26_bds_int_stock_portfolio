Main Data Flow

Bottom-to-top pipeline from data provenance to the seven-file endproduct.

L1 Alpaca OHLCV DownloadUpstream 1h OHLCV provenance.
↓
L2 ZIP Archive / Seed ExportOne archive per ticker, exported to data/seed/*_ohlcv_1h.parquet.
↓
L3 DuckDB Buildbuild_db.py loads all seed parquets into liora.duckdb.
↓
L4 Parquet 1h / 1d / 1wEach asset writes clean 1h OHLCV plus deterministic 1d and 1w roll-ups.
↓
L5 SplitWarmup, Train, and OOS windows with purge and embargo.
↓
L6 Features + Triple Barrier YCandidate side comes from 1h momentum. Features are resolved from 1h, 1d, 1w, and multi-timeframe namespaces. Y is ATR Triple Barrier.
↓
L7 Optuna HPO + KellyTrain-only XGB tuning and Train out-of-fold Kelly calibration.
↓
L8 XGB Strategy ArtifactFull-Train XGB is embedded as base64 in strategy_<TICKER>.py.
↓
L9 OOS EndproductOOS verdict, dashboard row, README, and the seven files per asset.
